Privacy Policy — My Friend

Last updated: June 28, 2026
Data controller: Manuel · manuel.adames88@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains what information the My Friend app collects, how it is used, where it is stored, and the choices you have. My Friend is a personal finance app that helps you track accounts, transactions, debts, budgets, savings goals, and related financial information.

The app is designed to collect as little personal data as possible and to keep your financial information private to you.

The short version

  • Your financial data is yours. It is not sold, shared with advertisers, or used to profile you.
  • The app contains no advertising, no analytics, no tracking, and no crash-reporting.
  • Your data is stored in your own private account and protected so that no other user can read it.
  • A small number of third-party services help the app function; they are all listed below.
  • You can request access to, or deletion of, your data at any time.

1. Information collected

Account / authentication

To create an account and sign in, the app collects your email address and a password. Your password is handled and stored in hashed form by the authentication provider (Supabase); it is never stored or seen in plain text.

Financial information you enter

The app stores the information you choose to enter: accounts and balances, transactions (amounts, descriptions, notes, dates, categories), statements, debts, payables, budgets, savings goals, investments, subscriptions, and the names or labels of people you track money with (for example, to split an expense). This is stored in your private, access-controlled account so it is available across your devices.

Voice input (optional)

If you use the voice entry feature, the app records a short voice note and converts it to text to pre-fill a transaction for you to review.

  • Transcription: where your device supports on-device speech recognition, the audio is transcribed on your device and does not leave it. Where on-device recognition is unavailable, the audio is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition service.
  • Interpreting the text: the transcribed text is sent to OpenRouter (an AI service) to suggest a structured transaction. To protect your privacy, the app does not send the names of people you track, the identifiers of your accounts, or any of your stored balances or transaction history — only the transcribed text and a list of your category and account names so the suggestion can match your setup. The names of people are matched to your contacts locally on your device.

You always review and confirm a voice-suggested transaction before it is saved. The feature is optional; if you do not use it, no audio or transcript is ever sent anywhere.

Information not collected

The app does not collect analytics, usage tracking, advertising identifiers, location data, or crash reports. It contains no advertising or third-party tracking technology.

2. How your information is used

Your information is used only to provide the core features of the app (storing and displaying your finances across your devices), to authenticate you and keep your account secure, and — where you choose to use it — to interpret voice input into a draft transaction. It is not used for advertising or profiling, and is not sold or rented to anyone.

Your data is stored in a private, access-controlled database. A small set of third-party sub-processors operate the app:

Your financial data is stored in the United States — East US (North Virginia), AWS us-east-1 region. Each provider processes data under its own privacy policy and security commitments. Apart from these providers, your data is not shared with any third party except where legally required.

4. How your data is protected

  • Your data is stored in a private account protected by row-level security, so each user can only ever access their own data. This is enforced at the database level and has been tested to confirm one user cannot read another user's information.
  • All communication between the app and its servers is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • The app stores a local copy of your data on your device so it works smoothly; this local copy is cleared when you sign out.

5. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account or ask for your data to be deleted, your information is removed from the database. Some information may persist briefly in backups before being overwritten in the normal course of operation.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the EU/UK GDPR or California's CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. The app does not sell personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact manuel.adames88@gmail.com.

7. Children's privacy

My Friend is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.
